What is this website about?

Sometimes, you have a bad day, but you don't have anybody you can, or want to, tell about it. That's where we come in.

At Bad_Day, you can vent anonymously and read the vents of other people. If you think somebody had a worse day than you, you can hit the thumbs down button. If you think your day was worse, you can hit the thumbs up. Each day, a worst day is crowned.

Of course, we have no way of vetting these stories. However, if you want to amuse yourself by lying to strangers for false sympathy, you should go elsewhere.
